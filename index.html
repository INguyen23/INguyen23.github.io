Problem Statement

Who is experiencing the problem? • Many college students are struggling with their course material. What is the problem? • The problem is that some students may need extra help or an easier resource to reach out to for academic school that the school cannot provide. Where does it present itself? • This problem presents itself across hundreds of colleges all across the country. Education is extremely difficult for many people and can require extra resources and time. Why does it matter? • This problem matters because a good education serves as a confining factor in one’s future. It sets up the basis for the next years of one’s life and struggling with school is an issue we can help.
